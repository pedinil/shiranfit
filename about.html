    We Create, Design and Make it Real

    SHIRANFIT is the Industry leader in the IRAN health and fitness industry. Formed in 2018 with a head office in Iran, Tehran. SHIRANFIT provides equipment, international training programs, consultancy services and continuous instructor training. SHIRANFIT are recognized as the leading voice in the IRAN health & fitness industry through our representation and licensing of leading health & fitness brands from across the globe. Currently SHIRANFITprovides World Class Fitness Solutions & products to over 450 high profile fitness clubs, hotels, schools, sporting institutions and supplies 140+ sporting goods retailers in the region. SHIRANFIT has also founded "SHIFIT" the regions largest annual Health & Fitness event providing continued education for instructors, personal trainers, group fitness instructors and club management alongside showcasing the latest in product innovation within the industry. SHIRANFIT will continue to expand its product portfolio and business strategies into the future. HISTORY 2012: Kamran Barahang arrive in Belgium to set up a chain of fitness clubs – returned investment in 9 months and seen the opportunity for fitness in the region. 2013 : SHIRANFIT (Iran Fitness Professionals) founded by Kamran Barahang Started by selling group fitness International programs. Working with EAS international as the Iran head office for distribution of nutrition products & Body-for-LIFE program. 2014: Launched the regions 1st fitness summit – SHIFIT Health & Fitness Summit. 2015: SHIFITPRO launched a new division focusing on functional training products. An education and training division was also launched to support this division. 2016: SHIFITPRO started servicing Fitness into Europe. 2016: Professional Fitness opened office in Europe , Europe for distribution of products, services and professional fitness education. 2017: SHIFITPRO open the regions first stand alone training academy. SHIFITPRO is now working with over 250 clubs in the IRAN region. 2017: MEFITPRO signs the 20th World class brand to its portfolio and has grown to a team of 25 staff at its head quarters in Iran, 2018: SHIFITPRO launches their latest division G.S.S (Global Sales System) which will further help fitness clubs become more successful in the region. SHIFITPRO launches BORN TO MOVE a series of fitness classes for young people aged 2-16 years. Inspiring a love of movement in the next generation. Awarded top distributor award from Triggerpoint / IMPLUS for launch into retail market Growth in regional distribution of products continues and we opened our 2nd warehouse in Tehran and launched 3PL in the in other cities 2019: SHIFITPRO continues to grow at double digit growth year on year and now services over 400 clubs regionally in 8 countries and over 150 retail points of sale. Awarded top Distributor award from SKLZ for global sales and business growth. Awarded for 2nd place global sales from Les Mills international for club license growth.
